Research

I study biological responses to trauma and prehospital interventions. In other words, who gets sick following traumatic injury, and why? How can we quickly identify these patients and adapt their care in order to improve outcomes? I leverage ground and helicopter emergency medical services (EMS) information, clinical data, and biological time series measurements. This work is mentored by investigators from the Department of Surgery and Department of Emergency Medicine.
