Research

I study traumatic injury and prehospital resuscitation. We leverage ground and helicopter emergency medical services (EMS) information, continuous waveform measurements, clinical data, multi-omics approaches, and markers of immune function and tissue damage to improve outcomes for injured patients. Recent publications: Google Scholar.
