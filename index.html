Research

I study the body's response to traumatic injury and prehospital resuscitation. In other words, who gets sick following trauma, and why? How can we adapt patient care to improve outcomes? To this end, we leverage ground and helicopter emergency medical services (EMS) information, continuous waveform measurements, clinical data, multi-omics approaches, and markers of immune function and tissue damage. This work is interdisciplinary, spanning emergency medicine, trauma surgery, and orthopaedic surgery. Our publications are listed on Google Scholar.
